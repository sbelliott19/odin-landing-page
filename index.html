These animals metaphorically symbolize our values
The beaver is said to be a spirit animal that represents the power of adaptability. This animal encourages people to be resourceful in their daily lives and to find creative solutions to problems they may face.
Bears hold significant spiritual meanings in many cultures, symbolizing courage, strength, protection, and maternal love. They are revered for their powerful and nurturing traits, representing spiritual power, harmony in nature, and intuition.
The tiger symbolizes the new and the unexpected. The tiger spirit animal teaches you to be more flexible and spontaneous in your daily life. It teaches you to leave room for the unknown in your life.
The dog spirit animal represents love, protection and loyalty. Dogs are faithful companions and reliable protectors who love their owners unconditionally. People who are attracted the dog totem are usually steady, kind and loving.
